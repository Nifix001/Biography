Personal Bio

Hey there, I'm Fawaz, I love the world of web development. Creating cool and user-friendly websites. It's my way of bringing ideas to life online. I'm still getting the hang of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a bunch of web development tools.

Outside the computer screen, I'm a huge football fan. Whether it's watching big European games or playing with friends at the local pitch, I'm all about the passion, strategy, and unity of the game. You'll often find me chatting about matches, breaking down player performances, and cheering for my favourite teams.

Beyond that, my African roots have given me a deep love for Afrobeat music. The infectious beats and storytelling in Afrobeat always hit the right note for me. I enjoy exploring the different sounds of African music and discovering new artists who add to the genre's ever-changing landscape.

I believe in the power of learning and growth. Whether it's books, science, or deep thoughts, I'm always looking to broaden my horizons and see things from different angles.

Web development, football, Afrobeat, and a curiosity for all things - that's what makes my world go round. I'm excited to keep learning, connecting with people who share my interests, and adding my bit to the ever-changing worlds of tech, culture, and knowledge.
